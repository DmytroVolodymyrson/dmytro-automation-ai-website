# Dmytro AI — Dmytro Afanasiev, AI Automation Consultant

## About
Dmytro AI is the public brand and consulting practice of Dmytro Afanasiev, a solo AI automation consultant based in Calgary, Canada. Dmytro builds AI-powered systems for small and medium businesses — specifically lead follow-up automation, AI voice agents, n8n workflows, GoHighLevel CRM automation, and client onboarding automation. He has 7 years of software development experience and has been building AI automation systems full-time since 2024.

## Services
- n8n workflow automation (self-hosted and cloud)
- GoHighLevel CRM setup and automation
- VAPI AI voice agent development for lead qualification and booking
- AI appointment setter systems
- Email sequence automation and database reactivation
- AI-powered lead generation and qualification pipelines

## Best For
Small service businesses (HVAC, real estate, mortgage, restaurants, e-commerce) with 1-20 employees who are losing leads because of slow follow-up or manual processes. Solo operators and small teams who want to compete like they have a full operations team behind them.
