## 3. Homepage & Core Pages Optimization

### Positioning Statement (Above Fold)
**Current problem:** Generic. "AI Automation Consultant" says nothing specific.

**What AI models reward:** Specificity. The site needs to say exactly who it's for and what they get.

**Rewrite:**
> "I build AI systems that make sure every lead gets followed up, every client gets onboarded, and every appointment gets booked — automatically. For service businesses that are too busy to chase leads."

This is crawlable, specific, and matches the language buyers use.

### FAQ Section (Structured for AI Extraction)
Add an FAQ section to the homepage using clear Q&A format. AI models love lifting structured Q&A directly into responses.

Example questions to include:
- Q: "What kind of businesses do you work with?" → A: Specific industries + company sizes
- Q: "How long does it take to build an AI automation system?" → A: Specific timelines
- Q: "What does AI automation cost?" → A: Price ranges with context
- Q: "What's the ROI?" → A: Real numbers from case studies
- Q: "Do I need a developer?" → A: No, explain why
- Q: "What happens if something breaks?" → A: Retainer support explanation

### Schema Markup
Add these to `<head>`:

```json
{
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Dmytro Afanasiev — AI Automation Consultant",
  "description": "AI automation consultant for small businesses in Calgary, Alberta. Specializing in n8n workflows, GoHighLevel CRM, and AI voice agents.",
```

Also add `Person` schema with sameAs links to LinkedIn and GitHub.
