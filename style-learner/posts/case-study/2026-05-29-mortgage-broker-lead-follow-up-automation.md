### Title
Mortgage Broker Lead Follow-Up Automation

### Subtitle
How a mortgage brokerage can stop losing expensive borrower inquiries by automating speed-to-lead, stale lead reactivation, document reminders, and client-management follow-up

### Case Study Summary Card
Industry: Mortgage Broker
Challenge: Mortgage leads and half-finished applications were going cold because follow-up, document collection, and broker handoff depended on manual reminders.
Solution: Automated speed-to-lead, incomplete-application reminders, document collection prompts, and CRM stage visibility for the brokerage.
Key Result: Faster borrower follow-up.

### Outcome
The brokerage gets a cleaner client-management rhythm: new inquiries are answered faster, dormant borrowers are reactivated, missing documents are chased automatically, and brokers can see which files need human attention.
