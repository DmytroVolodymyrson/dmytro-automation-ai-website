## Final Published Text

Title: CRM & Lead Follow-Up Automation for WheelsFeels
Subtitle: How WheelsFeels, a Houston-made vehicle camping systems brand, went from manually chasing 5,600 leads to automated follow-up for every serious inquiry

Metrics:
- Leads Organized & Enrolled: 5,600+
- Follow-Up Response Time: Manual → Instant
- Team Capacity Increase: 3x

Challenge:
1. WheelsFeels had accumulated over 5,600 leads across three years of operation. None of them had received systematic follow-up. Every inquiry that didn't convert immediately just sat in a spreadsheet.
2. Their team was spending hours each week manually emailing leads one by one, and still only reaching a fraction of them. High-intent vehicle camping system inquiries were getting the same treatment as contacts from two years ago.
3. There was no visibility into what was working. No tracking, no segmentation, no reporting. Just a growing pile of missed opportunities.

Solution:
1. Built a Supabase CRM that centralized all 5,600+ leads and segmented them into three pools based on source, recency, and engagement history. Each pool gets different follow-up logic.
2. Designed and deployed n8n workflows that automatically enroll new leads into personalized email sequences the moment they hit the CRM. Multi-account sending keeps deliverability high and volume manageable.
3. Connected the system to Slack so the sales team gets real-time alerts on high-intent leads, replies, and daily performance reports — without logging into any dashboard.
4. Built Google Sheets reporting that tracks every lead's status, email opens, and stage in the funnel. The owner sees exactly where each lead is without touching the system.

How It Works: Lead Enters CRM → Auto-Segment → Personalized Email Sequence → Team Alert on Reply

Tech Stack: n8n, Supabase, GoHighLevel, Slack, Google Sheets

Results: 100% of leads systematically followed up, Instant response time, +185% conversion rate lift (industry benchmark for similar implementations)
