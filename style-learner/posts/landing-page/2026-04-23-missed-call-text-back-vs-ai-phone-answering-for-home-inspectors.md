Heading: Missed Call Text-Back vs. AI Phone Answering for Home Inspectors
Body: If you miss calls during inspections, the real question is not whether automation sounds modern. It is whether a lighter missed-call text-back workflow is enough or whether you need live AI phone answering. Text-back is the narrower fallback: the call is missed, an immediate SMS goes out, and the buyer or agent gets a clear next step before the opportunity dies. AI phone answering is the heavier layer: the call gets answered live, routine scheduling and timing questions get handled in the moment, and higher-context conversations get routed back to a human with useful context instead of a vague voicemail. The right choice depends on how often callers truly need a live answer, how much agent trust depends on responsiveness, how much during-inspection demand you lose, and whether your business can reliably close the loop after a text thread starts.
