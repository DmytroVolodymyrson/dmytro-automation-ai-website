### Hero
Heading: Missed Call Text-Back for Medical Clinics
Body: Many medical clinics do not need a full live AI conversation every time the phone rings. They need a fast, credible response before the patient moves on. A prospective new patient calls during a check-in rush, a lunch break, or ten minutes after the office closes. Nobody answers, the call drops into voicemail, and by the time staff calls back the patient may already have tried two other clinics. Missed-call text-back is the lighter fallback layer between doing nothing and deploying full live AI phone coverage. The call is missed, a useful text goes out immediately, the patient gets a clear next step, and the clinic gets enough context to call back or route the appointment opportunity before it goes cold.
