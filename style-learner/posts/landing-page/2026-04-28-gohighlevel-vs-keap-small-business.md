### Hero
Heading: GoHighLevel vs Keap for Small Business
Body: GoHighLevel and Keap both promise to give a small business CRM, automations, landing pages, forms, scheduling, and follow-up in one place. But they solve that job differently. GoHighLevel is the flatter-priced all-in-one growth stack: lower entry price, broader built-in lead-follow-up tooling, and more room if you need unlimited users, contacts, or agency-style account structure. Keap is the older small-business CRM path: higher base cost, more traditional SMB CRM feel, stronger built-in emphasis on invoices and payments, and a workflow many long-time Infusionsoft users already understand. The real decision is not just which feature list looks longer. It is whether your business needs the cheaper all-in-one follow-up stack, the more traditional CRM/payments environment, or neither because the real problem is workflow design rather than software.
