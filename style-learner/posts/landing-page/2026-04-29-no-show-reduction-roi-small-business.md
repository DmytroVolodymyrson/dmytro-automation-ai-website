### FAQ
Heading: How do I estimate ROI on no-show reduction automation?
Body: Start with how many appointments no-show or cancel too late each month and what one recovered slot is worth. Then add some saved admin time from fewer manual reminders, cleaner reschedules, and better attendance visibility.

Heading: How quickly can no-show reduction automation pay for itself?
Body: For many appointment-driven small businesses, payback can happen within one to three months if a few saved appointments already carry real value.

Heading: How is this different from the broader scheduling and reminder ROI question?
Body: This page is narrower. It assumes the appointment already exists and focuses on attendance protection after booking — confirmations, reminder timing, reschedules, and no-show recovery.

Heading: What usually kills ROI on no-show reduction?
Body: Low appointment volume, already-low no-show rates, weak booking-confirmed triggers, or trying to solve an earlier-funnel lead-response problem with an attendance workflow.
