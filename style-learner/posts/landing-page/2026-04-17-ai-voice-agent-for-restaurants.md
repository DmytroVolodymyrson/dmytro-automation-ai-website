### What an AI voice agent can actually do for a restaurant
Intro: Practical explanation, not hype.
- Answer every inbound call: covers after-hours and service-time overflow.
- Book and confirm reservations: collects party size, date, time, and confirms the booking flow.
- Handle common questions instantly: hours, location, parking, private events, reservation policy.
- Capture missed demand after hours: callers still get an answer and a follow-up path.
- Route edge cases to a human: complaints, VIPs, private events, complicated requests.
- Reduce front-of-house interruptions: fewer repetitive calls during service.

### AI voice agent vs voicemail vs a human host
Comparison table across after-hours coverage, reservation capture, routine question handling, labor cost, and best use.

### Good fit / not the right fit
Good fit: missed calls during rush, meaningful after-hours reservation demand, repetitive phone questions, simple standardized booking flow, one extra booked table per day matters.
Not a fit: mostly walk-in concept, low call volume, highly customized concierge booking, constantly changing policies, expecting AI to replace every human interaction, weak demand rather than weak phone coverage.
