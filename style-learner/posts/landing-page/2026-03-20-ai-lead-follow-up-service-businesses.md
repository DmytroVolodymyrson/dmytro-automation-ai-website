### The follow-up problem
Intro: Lead follow-up for service businesses is broken in predictable ways.
- Leads go cold before anyone responds: A potential customer fills out your form at 9 PM. Your team sees it at 9 AM. By then, they have already called your competitor.
- Manual follow-up is inconsistent: When things get busy, follow-up slips. Some leads get three touches, some get none.
- No follow-up sequence after the first contact: Most leads do not convert on the first touch. They need two, three, sometimes five follow-ups before they book.
- Leads fall through the cracks between tools: A lead comes in through the website form, another through Instagram DM, another by phone. Without a unified system, some get lost entirely.

### What AI lead follow-up actually does
Intro: It is not a chatbot sitting on your website. It is a system that handles the full follow-up sequence from first contact through booking.
- Instant first response: Every lead gets contacted within seconds. SMS, email, or both.
- Multi-touch follow-up sequences: Structured sequence over the next days. Different messages, different angles, different channels.
- Lead qualification: Qualifying questions before booking. Service type, budget range, timeline, location.
- Multi-channel outreach: SMS, email, and web chat all handled from one system.
- CRM logging and tracking: Every interaction logged automatically. Lead status, conversation history, qualification data.
- Handoff to your team: Qualified leads get booked or routed to the right person with full context.

### What the numbers typically look like
- Response time: 2 to 48 hours → Under 60 seconds
- Lead contact rate: 30 to 50% → 85 to 95%
- Follow-up consistency: 1 to 2 touches → 5 to 7 touches over 14 days
- Booking rate from inquiries: 10 to 20% → 25 to 40%

### FAQ
- How fast should lead follow-up actually be? Under five minutes is the benchmark. Research from Lead Response Management shows contacting a lead within five minutes is 21 times more effective than waiting 30 minutes.
- Will AI follow-up feel impersonal to my leads? Done right, no. Modern AI follow-up pulls in the lead's name, the service they asked about, and specific details from their inquiry.
- What channels does AI lead follow-up cover? SMS, email, web chat, and in some cases voice.
- How does this work with my existing CRM? AI follow-up systems connect to most CRMs through APIs or integration platforms like n8n or Zapier.
- What does it cost to set this up? A one-time build typically runs $2,500 to $7,000 depending on complexity. Ongoing platform costs are usually $50 to $300/month.
- Can I still have my team follow up manually for big opportunities? Absolutely. The system handles the initial response and routine follow-up sequences. High-value leads get flagged and routed to your team with full context.
