Body: Customer service automation makes sense for e-commerce brands when support volume is high enough that order-status questions, returns, product questions, and post-purchase issues are clogging the same inbox and delaying real revenue conversations. The point is not to hide support behind a bot. The point is to make sure every request is captured, tagged, routed, and followed up consistently so customers get a faster answer and the team is not triaging the same chaos manually every day. For brands with repeat buyers, higher-ticket products, or a lot of pre-sale and post-sale questions, that usually creates cleaner operations fast.
