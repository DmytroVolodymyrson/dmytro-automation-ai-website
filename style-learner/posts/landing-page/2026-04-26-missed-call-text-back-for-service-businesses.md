### Hero
Heading: Missed Call Text-Back for Service Businesses
Body: Most service businesses miss 20-40% of inbound calls. During jobs, after hours, on weekends — the phone rings and nobody picks up. The standard fallback is voicemail, and most callers do not leave one. They hang up and call the next provider. Missed call text-back is the simplest layer of defense: the call is missed, an SMS goes out within seconds, the caller gets a clear next step, and the lead stays alive long enough for your team or booking flow to recover it. It is not a full phone system replacement. It is a fast, cheap, SMS-first recovery layer that sits between doing nothing and deploying a live AI voice agent.
