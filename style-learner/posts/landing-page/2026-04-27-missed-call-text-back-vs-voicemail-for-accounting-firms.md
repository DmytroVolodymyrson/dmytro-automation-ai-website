### Hero
Heading: Missed Call Text-Back vs. Voicemail for Accounting Firms
Body: If your accounting firm misses calls, missed-call text-back is usually a better fallback than voicemail when the real problem is consultation-request recovery during tax season, client meetings, lunch gaps, or after hours. Voicemail asks the caller to leave details, wait for a callback, and trust that someone will respond before they call the next CPA or bookkeeping firm. Missed-call text-back is not magic, but it does one important thing voicemail does not: it answers the miss immediately and gives the prospect or client a live next step while the request is still warm. The real decision is not whether texting feels newer. It is whether your firm needs a lighter recovery layer for missed calls or whether voicemail and callbacks are already quietly costing intake opportunities and client trust.
