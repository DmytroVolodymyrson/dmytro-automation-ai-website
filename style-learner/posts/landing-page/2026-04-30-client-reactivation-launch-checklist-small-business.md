### Hero
Heading: Client Reactivation Launch Checklist for Small Business
Body: Client reactivation should not go live just because the first draft email sounded friendly and the automation technically sent a test message. Before a small business starts dormant-client outreach, the operating layer under the campaign needs to be trustworthy: the first segment is actually clean enough to contact, recent and unhappy contacts are suppressed, timing matches a believable next service window, replies route to the right person with enough context, and the CRM shows what happened after the outreach fired. If those checks are still fuzzy, launch week usually teaches the wrong lesson. The wrong past customers get nudged, recent clients hear from you too soon, replies land in a shared inbox with no owner, and the team starts treating client reactivation like another cleanup project instead of a repeat-revenue workflow.
