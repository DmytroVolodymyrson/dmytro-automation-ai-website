Heading: Missed Call Text-Back ROI for Small Business
Body: Missed-call text-back usually has faster payback than heavier phone automation because it solves one narrow leak: a caller rings, nobody answers, and the opportunity dies before the team can react. If an automatic text keeps even a small share of those callers engaged long enough to book, request an estimate, or reply with what they need, the economics can work quickly. But the ROI is not automatic. It depends on whether the business truly loses good demand to missed calls, whether callers are willing to continue by text, and whether the team actually follows through once the thread starts.
