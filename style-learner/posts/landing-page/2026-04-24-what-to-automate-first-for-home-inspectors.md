### Hero
Heading: What to Automate First for Home Inspectors
Body: If your home inspection business knows it needs better automation, the first move usually should not be a giant all-in-one system. It should be one bounded workflow that fixes the leak already costing you referrals, bookings, or follow-through. For most home inspectors, that first workflow is one of four things: lead follow-up on new agent and buyer inquiries, missed-call recovery while you are still on-site, inspection scheduling and reminders once the booking is real, or report-delivery follow-up after the inspection is done. The right choice depends on where the business is already losing momentum today — before contact happens, while the phone is unanswered, during booking friction, or after the field work is complete.
