### Hero
Heading: Booking Confirmation and Reminder Setup: Hire Help or DIY?
Body: DIY can be enough when your booking flow is simple, your calendar rules rarely change, and one person can reliably test confirmations, reminder timing, reschedules, prep steps, and backfill rules without letting edge cases leak into live customer communication. It stops being enough when reminders keep firing for the wrong appointment, canceled slots never trigger a refill path, reschedules leave customers inside the old sequence, or the office loses trust because nobody can explain which booking state is actually true. The real decision is not whether booking reminders matter. It is whether your business can own the workflow discipline needed to make them dependable.
Sub: Below: when DIY is fine, when expert setup is worth paying for, which workflow layers usually break first, and how to decide before a narrow booking-confirmation project turns into an expensive scheduling cleanup.
