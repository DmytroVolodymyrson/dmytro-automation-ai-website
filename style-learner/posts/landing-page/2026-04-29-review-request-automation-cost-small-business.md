Heading: Review Request Automation Cost for Small Business
Body: Most small service businesses should expect a focused review-request build to land between $1K and $3.5K, with monthly running costs usually in the $15-$80 range depending on how cleanly completed jobs can be detected, whether the workflow needs complaint routing before the public ask, and how much CRM or human-handoff logic the post-job stage requires. The useful question is not just "what does review automation cost?" It is "what part of the post-job reputation stage am I actually trying to fix?" A business that only needs one clean review ask after every completed job should not buy the same system as a business that needs timing by service type, soft satisfaction checks, unhappy-reply routing, field-to-office handoff, and visibility into which jobs are producing reviews and which are not. This page breaks down realistic review-request pricing specifically — not broader lead-follow-up or quote-stage pricing, and not a full CRM or reputation-management rebuild — so a small business can budget the narrowest build that turns completed work into cleaner local-trust signals.
