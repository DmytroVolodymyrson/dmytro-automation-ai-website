### Hero
Heading: Intake and Conflict-Check Automation for Law Firms
Body: A lot of law firms do not lose momentum because the consultation itself is weak. They lose it before the consultation is actually ready to happen. The prospect said yes to the call, but nobody collected the right party names yet. The intake form came back half-finished. A receptionist or paralegal is still chasing the same background details over email. Conflict-check prep is sitting in three different places, and the attorney walks into the consult without the basics organized. Intake and conflict-check automation for law firms fixes that narrower pre-consult workflow. It gives the firm a cleaner way to gather the right facts earlier, surface missing information quickly, route secure next steps, and make sure consultations start with less admin drag and fewer avoidable surprises.
