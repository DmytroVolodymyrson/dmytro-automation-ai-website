Heading: Missed Call Text-Back vs. AI Phone Answering for Pool Service Companies
Body: If your pool service company keeps missing calls, the real decision is not whether automation sounds modern. It is whether a lighter missed-call text-back workflow is enough or whether you need live AI phone answering. Text-back is the narrower fallback: the call is missed, an immediate SMS goes out, and simpler service or callback intent gets recovered before the homeowner calls the next company on the list. AI phone answering is the heavier option: the call gets answered live, routine service-area and scheduling questions get handled on the spot, and basic intake starts during the call instead of waiting for a text thread later. The right choice depends on how often callers truly need a real answer now, how much demand dies during route-heavy days and opening season, how valuable after-hours and equipment-emergency calls are, and whether the team can reliably close the loop once a message conversation starts.
