## Style Notes

- This was a crawl-priority/internal-linking pass, not a net-new content page.
- The copy stays practical and buyer-oriented: “start here,” “proof before picking,” “choosing an automation project.”
- The link blocks are explicit for crawlers but still useful to humans deciding what to read next.
- Avoided fake claims about indexing or results. The language says Google has discovered pages, not that Google has indexed or ranked them.
- Kept proof references bounded to existing case-study assets and existing commercial/category pages.
