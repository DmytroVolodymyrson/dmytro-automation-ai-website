### Hero
Heading: Missed Call Text-Back vs. Voicemail for Medical Clinics
Body: If your medical clinic misses inbound patient calls during check-in rushes, between exam-room handoffs, at lunch, or after hours when someone still wants to book, missed-call text-back is usually a better fallback than voicemail. Voicemail asks the caller to explain themselves, wait, and trust that your callback will come fast enough to beat the next clinic they try. Missed-call text-back does one thing voicemail does not: it acknowledges the miss immediately and gives the patient a clear next step while their intent is still warm. The real decision is not whether texting feels more modern. It is whether your clinic needs a lighter recovery layer for missed patient calls or whether voicemail and delayed callbacks are already leaking booked visits and trust.
