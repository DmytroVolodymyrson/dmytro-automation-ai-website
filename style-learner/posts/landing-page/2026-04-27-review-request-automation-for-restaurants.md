Heading: Review Request Automation for Restaurants
Body: Most restaurants do not have a review problem. They have a post-visit follow-up problem. The guest leaves happy, the meal was good, the private event went smoothly — and then nobody asks. Review request automation for restaurants fixes the narrow workflow between a completed visit and a public reputation signal.
