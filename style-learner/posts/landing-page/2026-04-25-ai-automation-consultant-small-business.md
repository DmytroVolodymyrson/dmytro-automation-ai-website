# Updated FAQ
- Are there any hidden costs? No. I quote fixed prices for the build. You'll also have small monthly platform costs (like n8n hosting or CRM fees) which we'll discuss during the mapping stage. I optimize for the lowest long-term cost, often replacing expensive tools with self-hosted alternatives like n8n.
- How do you handle security and privacy? I prioritize data security by using official APIs and enterprise-grade platforms (Supabase, n8n, GoHighLevel).
