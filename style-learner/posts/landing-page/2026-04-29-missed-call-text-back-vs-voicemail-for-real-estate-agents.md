### Hero
Heading: Missed Call Text-Back vs. Voicemail for Real Estate Agents
Body: If your real estate business misses inbound calls while you are at a showing, in a listing appointment, driving between properties, or off-hours when buyers still want answers, missed-call text-back is usually a better fallback than voicemail. Voicemail asks the caller to explain themselves, wait, and trust that your callback will come fast enough to beat the next agent they try. Missed-call text-back does one thing voicemail does not: it acknowledges the miss immediately and gives the lead a clear next step while their intent is still warm. The real decision is not whether texting feels modern. It is whether your business needs a lighter recovery layer for missed buyer and seller calls or whether voicemail and delayed callbacks are already leaking showings, listing conversations, and trust.
