Heading: Client Onboarding Launch Checklist for Small Business
Body: An onboarding workflow should not go live just because it works on test data. Before launch, a small business needs to verify the real operating layer: does the deal-won trigger fire reliably, does the sequence respect timing and completion gates, does the kickoff booking link go out only when intake is actually done, do credentials land correctly, are internal tasks routed to the right person, and what happens when a client stalls or gets a duplicate message? If those checks are vague, the first real clients through the system usually expose them the hard way.
